Hello, I'm Arupam

Welcome to my personal website! I am a Neuroscience Researcher at the Institute of Developmental Biology and Neurobiology (iDN) , Germany.

Neurological diseases are not only permanently debilitating but are the fastest-growing cause of death among non-communicable diseases. Strategies to promote repair of the nervous system are extremely rare, and to date, there are no therapies available.

At Johannes Gutenberg University Mainz , in collaboration with the Max Planck Institute for Polymer Research , our interdisciplinary team combines neurobiology, neuroimmunology, chemistry, and polymer research to develop new strategies for regenerative treatments for multiple sclerosis and traumatic brain and spinal cord injuries.

Beyond the lab, I enjoy cooking and exploring new places. If you share an interest in neurobiology, regenerative medicine, or the molecular mechanisms of brain repair, I would love to connect!
